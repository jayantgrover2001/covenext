FAQs

Common questions about our services and how we work

What services do you offer? +
We specialize in digital marketing and web development for local businesses. Our services include local SEO, Google Business Profile optimization, Google Ads management, custom website development, e-commerce solutions, and ongoing digital strategy consulting.
How long does it take to see results? +
The timeline varies depending on the service. For paid advertising like Google Ads, you can see results within weeks. SEO and organic strategies typically take 3-6 months to show significant results. Website development projects usually take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity.
What makes you different from other agencies? +
We focus exclusively on local businesses and understand the unique challenges you face. We combine technical expertise with local market knowledge, and we're committed to being your long-term partner, not just another vendor. Every strategy is customized to your specific market and business goals.
Do you require long-term contracts? +
For ongoing services like SEO and marketing management, we typically recommend a minimum 6-month commitment to see meaningful results. However, we're flexible and can discuss options that work for your business. One-time projects like website development don't require long-term contracts.
How much do your services cost? +
Pricing varies based on your specific needs and goals. SEO services start at $999/month, website development starts at $2,999, and Google Ads management starts at $599/month plus ad spend. We offer custom quotes based on your requirements and can work within various budgets.
Will I own my website? +
Absolutely! When we build your website, you own all the code, content, and assets. We'll provide you with all login credentials and documentation. You're never locked into working with us, though we hope you'll stay because of the value we provide.
Do you provide ongoing support? +
Yes! We offer ongoing support and maintenance packages for all our services. For marketing services, we provide regular reports and strategy updates. For websites, we offer maintenance plans that include updates, backups, security monitoring, and technical support.
What industries do you work with? +
We work with a wide range of local businesses including restaurants, HVAC companies, medical practices, legal firms, retail stores, home services, and more. If you serve a local market, we can help you grow your digital presence.
